sorry; more confused. "vsm for a (sw) app" ??. value-stream mapping is a technique to analyze *human* (and these days, robot) activities in their work stream (e.g creating a sw product, by humans), and classify the waste in the human activities. eg how much time is spent for a paying-customer request sitting in the prod mgmt group, how much time is spent creating intermediate artifacts, etc

https://www.amazon.com/Learning-See-Stream-Mapping-Eliminate/dp/0966784308

and more important than the details of the def, in terms of successful change: since the implications should be a total-org-system "concept to cash" improvement, it really needs to be done by very senior mgmt that have the authority to change the total system, and done in a way that they *own* the insight from doing the analysis themselves, and develop the shared understanding by doing it together amongst a senior group. *showing* them a computer diagram is a futile as showing them a system model (CLD), in terms of generating the owning, insight, and shared understanding that is key for successful deep change sustained with "constancy of purpose" by the senior mgmt.
