history of the first 2 LeSS books and miscellania: the major organization
of the first 2 LeSS books is: (1) *thinking* tools, (2) *org* tools, (3)
*action* tools. this structure was inspired from don reinertsen's "managing
the design factory" book structure. the "practices" book is really the
third section: action tools. also, it was originally going to be 1 book,
and read in the expected order, thinking, org, and action tools, because
that's how we view the order of either explaining or improving in LeSS. bas
suggested to me that we split it into 2 books as it would otherwise have
been 800 pages long ;)
