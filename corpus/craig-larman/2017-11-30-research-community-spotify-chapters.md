> I wonder how communities in LeSS compare to chapter and guilds in the
> Spotify model?

"communities" is the simple general term and concept, in the LeSS spirit of
KISS

a spotify "chapter" is a community limited to a "tribe" (similar to a LeSS
Huge RA)

a "guild" is a community across tribes; perhaps across the entire enterprise

we encourage simpler terms and less required structures than required by
"chapters & guilds"

> areas or that some areas have CoPs not present in others (something similar
> to the distinction between chapters and guilds)?

don't know the patterns of commonality. we do *not* recommend granular
communities at the RA level, because the concerns such as Architecture &
