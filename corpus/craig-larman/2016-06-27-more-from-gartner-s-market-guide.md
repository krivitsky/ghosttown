gartner sent me the report. background: 2 gartner folks attending my LeSS
course, in prep for the report, and afaik really dug it. their private
comments to me indicated no bias to 1 FW.

the report language is quite neutral and doesn't promote safe/etc. it's
essentially a "neutral" catalog of scaling FWs with summary data about it.
for example, this is the report recommendation:

"Determine key selection criteria, especially regarding degree and type of
scaling, organizational culture and the potential impact of change required
to implement the framework.
Consider the different approaches to scaling agile development, and create
a shortlist that corresponds to your organization's culture, maturity and
needs.
Look closely at the viability of the framework's training, consulting and
support network, as you may have to customize the enterprise agile
framework you adopt to ensure its acceptance in your organization."
