have a new one for me, just used in my LeSS keynote at bengaluru scrum
gathering: for large-room public speaking when group can't see a flipchart
or whiteboard, point (e.g.) mac & its camera at a flipchart. use (e.g.)
quicktime in "new movie" mode (but don't start recording). flipchart
flipped to "landscape mode" is better for quicktime. this photo from the
talk illustrates:
