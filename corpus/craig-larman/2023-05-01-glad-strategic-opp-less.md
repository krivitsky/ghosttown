GLAD [Generative-ai & LLM-assisted Dev] creates an opp for LeSS message &
market. it will disrupt existing narrow single-specialist roles. in 1000
futures, within 2 years, would bet 1/2 of equity at 2:1 odds, that in at
least 700 of those futures...

[most of this was the analysis from my 2020 LeSS keynote]

those clinging to single specialization will say (and articles will write)
"oh we will still have [front-end designers/dba/...] who will just do their
task more efficiently." ...

but just had a person in my Lisbon course who diid not know automated
testing and had allocated 20 person days for their creation; it took them
*one* PD, and the output was good. that's an ORDER OF MAGNITUDE reduction,
and this will be common.

in this new paradigm will eventually become clear that a person doing just
1 task is nonsensical, and/or people will rediscover lean-thinking when
they see their "front-end dev"/etc generating mountains of waste by rapidly
creating mountains more stuff in their single-task output (designs, front
end code, component3 code, ...) than is needed by customer demand. ie
rediscovery what *pull* really implies and why it's important.

all this invalidates traditional planning on the estimation of how long it
takes people to do things, and the variability around that. there will then
be an influence to strengthen feedback loops and shorten loop cycles. and
that will create an influence for people learning more things, and
adaptiveness... and GLAD of course make that much easier, and obviously so
to mgmt.

and then -- key point -- ODs ("agile scaling FWs") that are based on
single-specialization and traditional mgmt assumptions will increasingly be
seen as a mismatch for the new realties, and then *complicated* traditional
ODs (safe, etc) with lots of prescriptive advice will increasingly be seen
as a mismatch

... and that will create a chance for people to want to hear the idea of
* simplification* in OD, a less-complicated organization

therefore 2024++ is gonna create windows of opp for hearing these new/old
ideas again, growing the LeSS market

*encourage all the trainers & candidates to start to explore with clients
what GLAD implies in terms of OD, and how that naturally takes one towards
a simple OD with a focus on more learning & adapting, rather than many
narrow roles*

am gonna be giving presentations with titles such as "Impact of GLAD on
Management & Org Design"

this is the largest & most disruptive event in sw dev, ever. this is an
opportunity to make a crack in traditional (safe/etc) orgs and towards much
simpler, and we need people to clearly make this point, and advocate for it
