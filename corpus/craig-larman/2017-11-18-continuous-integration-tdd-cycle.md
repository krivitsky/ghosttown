for me, coming from the original XP view from kent of integrating
continuously and TDD, the goal was to "integrate every TDD cycle". in
smalltalk, kent, i, and others would use the VC and shared repo tool called
Envy

have been keeping informal stats for many years:

smalltalk with Envy: 1 TDD cycle avg: 2 min

java with SVN/Git: 3 min

c++: 6 min

these cycle times assume "steady state" dev, not during messy start up of
some new architectural area, when there's a lots of pauses to think/discuss
while clearing the design fog

so for me, that's the goal in the delay before integrating... that's as
close to "continuously" as possible
