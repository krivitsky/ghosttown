*=== MINDMAP + ROTATION ESPIONAGE*
step 1: in my course teams evolve a mindmap of the course objectives, once
per day (3 times total).

ed goal: review, highlight, synthesize, remember the big ideas

step 2: after 2nd evolution (day 2), each team does rotation espionage to
all the other maps, steals points they want to put on their map, and then
they return & update it

ed goal: looking at all the other maps, with a mind to seek new info,
reinforces the repeating common points they see, plus they collect points
they forgot. when they finally update their map, it is much richer, and the
learners have a more complete mental and visual model of the big ideas, and
it is a little more into their memory
