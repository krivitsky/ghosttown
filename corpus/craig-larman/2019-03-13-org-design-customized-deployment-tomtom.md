this is a followup case, just worked at: tomtom does maps & routing. they have public standalone products including a map app, a map handheld device (or at least they did), a map web service. (from a LeSS view, these can all be considered one product with variant I/O channels). the most interesting (gnarly) part to me in terms of org design -- same issue/question as in prior opening question -- is that they have many customized deployments, usually into diff cars.

and as described in me original post, they org this as deployment projects.

let's say they have 20 simultaneous customized deployments. each deployment group might be 20 people. each deployment might take 6-12 months (all those numbers may be somewhat off, but you get the idea).

all my prior post comments about the possible frictions are observed

am posting the same question again, because it is a kind of "wicked problem" with no neat solution as far as i can see or have seen, but it's a common case and an important org problem in large product groups. so it will benefit from input, stories, thoughts, fresh eyes, etc...

*QUES >> what are your observations and/or experiments in such orgs that you've seen?*
