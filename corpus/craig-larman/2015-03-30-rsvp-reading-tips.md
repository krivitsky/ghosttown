for those of you exploring other ways to read, try this experiment:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_serial_visual_presentation

* i use spreed, the chrome plugin. super easy for rvsp'ing a web page, but
also works with pasted text

* 100 pt font, so the eyes/brain have very low effort.

* i read at 600 wpm usually

* spreed has a "paste to spreed" function so that text in a pdf/epub/etc
can be pasted in. i use that to read books in epub or pdf originally

* big pdf select-all & copy? Apple Preview hangs, but Adobe Reader does the
job

* epub select-all & copy? the only epub reader i've found that does that on
the Mac is ClearView.

* my read batch size is 1 chapter. then stop. see next...

* because rvsp is so linear, to first start to form a mental model of the
chapter contents/hierarchy, i first look at the chapter outline. then when
rsvp'ing, the contents start to fit into that mental structure. then i'll
look at the outline again after rsvping. also tend to just let the
brain/mind relax without any deliberate activity for a few minutes after,
to let the text... sink in
