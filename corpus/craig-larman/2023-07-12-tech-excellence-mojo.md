in 3 years in 1000 futures would bet in 700+ of those mojo is influential
language & stack, esp for ai/ml dev. from chris lattner, easily the most
influential language designer & compiler writer of the last 20 years, and
arguably of all time. creator or co-creator of LLVM, Clang, & Swift

python has won the ai dev (and broadly "misc dev") mindshare & use. mojo is
to be a superset of python, intending to compile all existing python code
(huge advantage, big deal). and key point: compile, not interpret. current
tests show min 10x speedup, and it's just starting. some tests show
100-1000x

mojo is beta, not yet feature complete. will OSS after stabilizing

the other key to mojo is an architecture & set of modules that allows one
to deploy for training (and delivery) on misc and complex platforms without
concern for details... all abstracted away. eg "train on 10k cluster of
nvidia H100 on DGX platform." a big deal: today it can take literally
_months_ to set up a big model for training and then deployment on
platforms, due to the idiosyncratic complexity and scaling issues. mojo
aims to make this a non-issue with modules that handle & hide the
complexity across any and all training platforms

mojo will accelerate ai/ml dev and broaden its access to many more devs. it
will reduce the need for "ai/ml specialists" based on learning/knowing
idiosyncratic training-stack setup details
