Build
tony fadell is of course probably the most experienced hw/sw product
designer & mgr in the world. he wrote this to capture a summary of his
advice for next generations, for the book he wished he had had when
younger. it ranges from "build yourself" to build product to becoming a ceo
in a product company. ie guidance for the career journey in building
products. would strongly recommend this to people in startups or
product-focused companies.

Exactly What to Say
phil jones presents very concrete language tools useful for coaches,
consultants, educators. simple, practical
