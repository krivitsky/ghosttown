deferring the question, but picking up a coaching point:

i approach this analysis with clients by them first deciding on the
optimizing goal (e.g. global adaptiveness). then the evaluation of
product-slicing alternatives shifts from a "religious" discussion based on
opinions or methods, to a more basic analysis/logic question: is the
current slicing consistent with the optimizing goal? time and time again,
this approach quickly cuts thru so much fog and talk and opinion
