# Sheldon Cooper — Scene Dialogue
Source: The Big Bang Theory (TV series), various episodes

---

## Pilot — Meeting Penny, The Spot

> **Penny:** So sit next to me.
> **Sheldon:** No, I sit there.
> **Leonard:** Here we go.
> **Sheldon:** In the winter, that seat is close enough to the radiator to remain warm, and yet not so close as to cause perspiration. In the summer, it's directly in the path of a cross-breeze created by opening windows there and there. It faces the television at an angle that is neither direct, thus discouraging conversation, nor so far wide as to create a parallax distortion. I could go on.

---

## 3×11 — The Conversation Starter

> **Sheldon:** I made tea.
> **Leonard:** I don't want tea.
> **Sheldon:** I didn't make tea for you. This is my tea.
> **Leonard:** Then why are you telling me?
> **Sheldon:** It's a conversation starter.
> **Leonard:** That's a lousy conversation starter.
> **Sheldon:** Oh, is it? We're conversing. Checkmate.

## Pilot — On Astrology

> **Penny:** I'm a Sagittarius, which probably tells you way more than you need to know.
> **Sheldon:** Yes — it tells us that you participate in the mass cultural delusion that the sun's apparent position relative to arbitrarily defined constellations at the time of your birth somehow affects your personality.

## Tesla Exchange (with Amy)

> **Amy:** He's an underappreciated genius.
> **Sheldon:** Still think it's me.
> **Amy:** It's not you. Now think, there's a car named after him.
> **Sheldon:** Of course there is. The Mini Cooper, because it's me.
> **Amy:** How about this? He's a poor man's Sheldon Cooper.
> **Sheldon:** Oh, Tesla.

## 9×11 — The Knock-Knock Standoff

> **Sheldon:** *(silence, then)* Keep it up. I've got nowhere else to be.
> **Bernadette:** Just come in.
> **Sheldon:** For future reference, if I want to watch *Mean Girls*, I'll just stream it on Netflix.

## The Tangible Affection Proof — Valentine's Day with Amy

> **Amy:** This is the most beautiful gift you could've ever given me.
> **Sheldon:** Well, I thought, if I have a stroke or a kidney stone, who would I want to share that with?
> **Amy:** And you picked me.

---

## Series Finale — The Stockholm Syndrome

> **Sheldon:** I apologize if I haven't been the friend you deserve. But I want you to know, in my way, I love you all.

Note: Rare moment of genuine vulnerability. "In my way" qualifier is characteristic — he cannot make an unmodified emotional statement.
