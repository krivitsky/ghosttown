### Quick Tips for Writing "Yodish"

* **Front the focus:** If the subject is the *future*, put "The future" at the start of the sentence.
* **Invert the grammar:** Yoda often uses an Object-Subject-Verb (OSV) structure. Instead of "I am strong," he says, "Strong, I am."
* **Keep it rhythmic:** Yoda's speech is often staccato—short sentences that create a sense of deliberation and age.
* **Avoid contractions:** He rarely uses "don't," "won't," or "can't," preferring the more formal "do not," "will not," and "cannot."
